== Description ==

**EFFORTLESSLY SYNC CONTACT FORM 7 FORM FIELDS WITH INSIGHTLY MODULES AND AUTOMATICALLY CREATE INSIGHTLY OBJECTS AND MAINTAIN A DETAILED FORM SUBMISSION LOG THAT YOU CAN DOWNLOAD AND DELETE IN BULK.**

**MWB CF7 Integration with Insightly** plugin allows the admin to associate CF7 form fields with Insightly modules. It sends all the CF7 form submission data over to the connected Insightly account and creates contacts, leads, tasks, etc.

Contact form 7 is a pretty useful marketing plugin. It allows you to collect crucial data using forms and add it to your customer database. This data is later used to lead targeted marketing campaigns and grow your email list. 

To effectively organize, segment, and manage your online business campaigns, you need Insightly CRM.

Our plugin effortlessly integrates CF7 with your Insightly account. It will help customize and automate your workflows and control the data users submit. Thus, optimizing your marketing efforts.

== WHAT’S INCLUDED IN OUR FREE CF7 INTEGRATION WITH INSIGHTLY PLUGIN: == 

**1)** **Integrate CF7 with Insightly Account**
Enter the API credentials (API key and Insightly API URL) to connect CF7 with your Insightly account. On the plugin’s dashboard, you can view the connection status. Plus, you can reauthorize and disconnect the integration at any time.




**2)** **Filter CF7 submissions As Per Feeds**
You can apply AND/OR conditions in the CF7 forms. Any form submissions that fail to meet the applied conditions, will be filtered out and their logs will not be created.  

**3)** **Detailed Log**
A log entry is automatically made upon all successful form submissions. You can expand the log entry and view the details of form submissions as well as the response from Insightly. 

**4)** **Delete Plugin Data On Uninstall**
You can enable the deletion of all plugin-related data from your WordPress database upon plugin uninstallation.

**5)** **Associate Form Fields with Insightly Modules**
Create CF7 forms and associate fields with Insightly field modules (contact, leads, organization, etc.) and sync the form submission data over to your Insightly CRM account.

**6)** **Clear And Download Logs**
The logs are automatically deleted after 7 days. But you can clear the logs in bulk when you don’t need them. However, for further analysis, you can also download them in bulk

**7)** **Insightly Custom Value**
While creating forms, you can assign Insightly custom values to the form fields. When you don’t have the required fields, you can add Insightly form field placeholders to create a new customized field

**8)** **Receive Error Email Notification**
You can enable email notification for any error that occurs in the API interaction during the form submissions. By default, you will receive notifications on your WordPress email.

**9)** **Delete Logs after N days**
You can schedule the bulk deletion of CF7 form submission logs but the number of days for deletion must be greater than 7.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==


= How will CF7 Integration with Insightly plugin help me? =
This plugin will help in sending all the data like form submissions entries over Insightly CRM as per the modules of Insightly. This can help in creating contacts, leads, tasks over Insightly.
 
= Can we schedule the deletion of logs? =
Yes, you can. To schedule the deletion, go to Settings >> Delete Logs After N Days, enter the number of days, and click on Save.

= After how many days can we delete the logs? Is there any time limit? =

The minimum number of days should be seven and after that, you can set your limit.

= What If we need the logs data on other devices for further study? =
In this case, you can simply download the logs. Go to the Logs tab and click on the Download button located in the upper right corner.

= Can we also delete the CF7 Integration with Insightly Plugin data? =
Yes, you can delete the plugin data. To enable this feature, go to Setting, click on the Plugin Data toggle, and click on Save.

= Is it possible to upload attachments in CF7 Integration with Insightly easily? =
  Yes, you can upload attachments to Insightly.
